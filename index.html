COOKIES

Cookies make a good afternoon treat.We got the best cookie recipe.

Types of cookies

  1. choco chip cookie
  2. cookie biscuits

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • eggs
  • butter
  • Yeast
  • sugar
  • Flour

Procedure

  1. Beat ,sugar,eggs,butter and yeast in one bowl;mix the flour and leaveners in another
  2. Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture
  3. Roll the dough into balls and bake until the edges are golden

  • cooling rack
